👋 About MindGuard

Empowering people to take control of their digital lives

Our Story

MindGuard was born from a simple observation: traditional website blockers were too easy to bypass and too frustrating to use. They treated users like they couldn't be trusted with their own decisions.

We believed there had to be a better way—one that respected your autonomy while helping you build better habits. That's why we created MindGuard: not just a blocker, but a focus companion that challenges you to think before you click.

The Problem We're Solving

In today's hyper-connected world, digital distractions are everywhere. Social media, streaming platforms, and endless content compete for your attention every second. Studies show the average person loses over 2 hours daily to mindless browsing.

But simply blocking websites doesn't address the root cause. You need tools that help you build awareness and make conscious choices about your time.

Our Approach

MindGuard uses AI to detect distracting content in real-time. When you try to access a blocked site, instead of showing a boring "Access Denied" message, we present you with an engaging microtask.

These challenges—from breathing exercises to brain teasers—make you pause and reflect: "Do I really need this right now?" Most of the time, the answer is no. And when it's yes, you've earned access mindfully.
