1. Introduction

MindGuard ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension and web services.

By using MindGuard, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Data: Email address, name, and password (encrypted)
  • Profile Settings: Focus hours, blocking preferences, role (student/professional/freelancer)

2.2 Usage Data

  • Blocked Domains: Website domains you visit (e.g., "youtube.com"), but NOT specific URLs or page content
  • Timestamps: When sites were blocked or accessed
  • Microtask Results: Whether you completed, failed, or skipped challenges
  • Focus Score: Your productivity metrics and statistics

2.3 What We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ Specific URLs or page content
  • ❌ Passwords or login credentials to other sites
  • ❌ Personal messages or emails
  • ❌ Payment information (we're free!)
  • ❌ Location data (except country for holiday detection)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide Services: Block distracting sites and track your progress
  • Personalization: Adjust blocking intensity based on your settings
  • Analytics: Generate productivity reports and insights
  • AI Training: Improve distraction detection algorithms (anonymized data only)
  • Communication: Send important updates (you can opt out)

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Where We Store Data

Your data is stored securely on Firebase servers (Google Cloud Platform) with industry-standard encryption. Data centers are located in the US and EU.

4.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • Authentication: Secure JWT tokens with expiration
  • Access Control: Strict database rules—only you can access your data
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security reviews and updates

4.3 Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all data at any time from Settings.

5. Third-Party Services

MindGuard uses these trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Database and authentication
  • OpenAI: AI-powered content analysis (anonymized)
  • Nager.Date API: Holiday detection (no personal data shared)

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Download all your data at any time
  • Correction: Update your profile information
  • Deletion: Permanently delete your account and all data
  • Portability: Export your data in JSON format
  • Opt-Out: Disable analytics or notifications

To exercise these rights, visit your Settings page or contact us at privacy@mindguard.com.

7. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies to:

  • Keep you logged in (session cookies)
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website performance (Google Analytics—anonymized)

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not work properly.

8. Children's Privacy

MindGuard is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be announced via email.

10. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Reach out to us:

  • Email: privacy@mindguard.com
  • Support: support@mindguard.com
  • Contact Form: Contact Page

Our Commitment: Your trust is everything to us. We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Period.
