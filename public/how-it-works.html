📖 How MindGuard Works

Your journey to better focus in 4 simple steps

1

🚀 Install the Extension

Add MindGuard to your Chrome browser with one click. No complex setup required—just install and you're ready to go. The extension is lightweight and won't slow down your browser.

2

⚙️ Configure Your Rules

Choose which websites to block or allow. You can manually add sites, or let MindGuard's AI automatically detect distracting content. Set your focus hours and customize blocking intensity based on your schedule.

3

🎯 Browse with Focus

When you try to visit a distracting site, MindGuard intervenes immediately. Instead of showing a boring block message, you'll face an engaging microtask challenge that makes you think twice.

4

📊 Track Your Progress

Watch your focus score grow as you build better habits. Get detailed analytics on your productivity, time saved, and distraction patterns. Celebrate milestones and stay motivated with AI-generated insights.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI blocking work? ▼
MindGuard uses machine learning to analyze website content, titles, and your browsing patterns. It assigns a risk score to each site and automatically blocks those likely to distract you from your goals.
Can I bypass the blocks in emergencies? ▼
Yes! You can complete a microtask to unlock the site temporarily. If it's truly urgent, you can also disable MindGuard from the extension settings, though this will affect your focus score.
Does it work on mobile? ▼
Currently, MindGuard is available as a Chrome extension for desktop browsers. We're working on mobile apps for iOS and Android, coming soon!
Is my data private? ▼
Absolutely! We only track the domains you visit (not specific URLs or page content). All data is encrypted and stored securely. We never sell your information to third parties.
What are microtasks? ▼
Microtasks are short challenges you must complete to unlock blocked sites. They include breathing exercises, quizzes, typing tests, riddles, and reflection prompts. They're designed to make you pause and consider if you really need to visit the site.
How much does it cost? ▼
MindGuard is completely free to use! We believe everyone deserves access to tools that improve their focus and productivity.
